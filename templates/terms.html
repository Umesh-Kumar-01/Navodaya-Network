Website Rules and Guidelines

User Conduct and Content Guidelines

  • Respectful and Supportive Community: Users must maintain a respectful and supportive environment, fostering positive interactions among Navodayans.
  • Relevant Content: Users are encouraged to share content relevant to the Navodaya community's interests and objectives.

Privacy and Data Protection

  • Public Data: Users should be aware that their public data, such as names and non-sensitive information, will be visible to everyone on the site, promoting transparency and community engagement.
  • Private Data: Users' private data, such as current occupation and location, will be used solely to send them specific requests or relevant information. This data will be kept confidential and not shared with other users.

Registration and User Accounts

  • Accurate Information: Users must provide accurate and up-to-date information during the registration process.
  • Account Security: Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their accounts and passwords.

Interactions and Networking

  • Collaborative Networking: Users are encouraged to engage in collaborative networking, sharing ideas, knowledge, and opportunities that benefit the Navodaya community as a whole.
  • Integrity: Users must conduct themselves with integrity, promoting honesty and trust within the community.

Liability and Disclaimers

  • Informal Nature: The website is an informal platform and not affiliated with any official Navodaya organization. Users should participate with an understanding of its voluntary and community-driven nature.
  • Limited Liability: The website and its administrators shall not be held liable for user-generated content or interactions.

Termination of Accounts

  • Grounds for Termination: Accounts may be terminated for serious violations of the website's rules or misuse of the platform that harms the community's spirit.

Updates to the Rules

  • Right to Modify: Reserve the right to modify the rules and guidelines as necessary, ensuring transparency about any changes.

Reporting and Moderation (Future Update)

  • Reporting Mechanism: A reporting feature will be implemented in the future to allow users to report content or behavior that violates the community guidelines.
  • Moderation: Future updates will include a moderation system to review and address reported content, ensuring the platform's positive environment.
